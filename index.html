Biography

Warm Bench brings you into an extraodinary musical unverse. Influenced by numerous music styles like roots, funk and soul. We will make you groove on brand new modern reggae music!

The band is composed by 8 musicians. This quite big family was born in 2014 from Damien and Marine's love for jamaican music. That passion made them decide to found a band where the offbeat makes your hips swing!
